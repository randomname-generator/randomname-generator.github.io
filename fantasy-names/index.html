Fantasy name generator — tips & examples

Searching for an inspiring fantasy name generator that produces readable yet mythical results? This page collects essential tips for medieval, elven, mage and warrior names, plus a curated list of 50 examples to spark ideas. Keep names short and pronounceable, test them out loud, and match the vibe of your world. For epic tales, old kingdoms and heroic quests, consistency matters more than complexity.

How to craft memorable medieval & elven names

  • Match culture & tone: elven names flow softly; warrior names feel sharp and strong.
  • Keep length in check: 4–12 characters is ideal for readability and quick recall.
  • Balance vowels & consonants: elven → more vowels; warrior → stronger consonant clusters.
  • Say it aloud: good names are easy to pronounce and remember.
  • Make it yours: combine a short seed with generated results to get unique blends.
